Sky Tycoon

Sky Tycoon is a manegment tycoon game similar to Adventure Capitalist. Where the player places building in order to generate gold. With diffrent buildings generating different amounts.
On this project, I was part of a group of 2 programmers. It was my first expirence working on a team in a professional enviroment.
